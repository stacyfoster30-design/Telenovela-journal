Telenovela Journal Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Telenovela Journal is a creative journaling app that helps users turn journal prompts, story ideas, and optional images into dramatic, telenovela-style scenes. This policy explains what information may be handled by the app.

Information you provide

The app may process content you choose to enter, such as journal text, story prompts, names used inside a story, and optional images or scene details. Please do not enter sensitive personal information unless you are comfortable having it processed for app functionality.

How information is used

User-provided content is used to generate, display, and improve the requested creative journal experience. Content may be sent to service providers used to operate app features, such as AI text or media generation, when those features are enabled.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the app, comply with law, prevent abuse, or protect users and the service.

Account, ads, and children

The app is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed to children under 13. The current app does not use third-party advertising. If sign-in, subscriptions, or additional provider features are added, this policy will be updated as needed.

Security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards for information processed through the app. User content is retained only as needed for app functionality, legal compliance, troubleshooting, or as controlled by the platform and service providers involved.

Your choices

You may stop using the app at any time. If you need help with privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us using the email below.
